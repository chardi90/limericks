Limericks

A limerick is a 5-line humorous poem with a bouncy rhythm. Choose four playful words that rhyme. e.g.

Tokyo, umbrella, gecko, slipped, sushi

Peru, canoe, kangaroo, flew, bamboo

Madrid, wig, squid, hid, lid

Rome, comb, gnome, roamed, dome
